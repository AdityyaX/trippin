About Imran Khan:

Imran Khan's music career began with his debut single, Ni Nachleh. His super hit singles "Amplifier" and "Bewafa", which received huge recognition on Youtube in their first week of release, brought him widespread popularity. ‘Unforgettable’, his debut album, which includes songs like "Aaja we Mahiya" and "Superstar", along with 11 other songs was also nominated for Best Album by the UK Asian Music Awards. His tracks like "Satisfya" and "Imaginary" created a new wave in the music industry. Supermoon is a flagship intellectual property of ZEE LIVE, a division of Zee Entertainment Enterprises Limited (ZEEL). Supermoon is a platform for LIVE original content that resonates with the fans of an artist. They have successfully conducted India’s largest concert touring format in the music and comic genre with legends like Russell Peters, Prateek Kuhad, Divine, Raftaar, B Praak, and Yohani, to name a few. Supermoon is known to package a magnificent experience that caters to the desires of a LIVE audience. Follow @supermoonhq channels to stay updated with new thrilling concerts/ shows.
